About Pasuseva

Pasuseva is a compassionate initiative focused on improving animal healthcare and empowering rural farmers across India.

We deliver essential veterinary services, conduct awareness drives, and provide hands-on training to support animal welfare and sustainable rural development.

Our mobile veterinary units and periodic health camps have already reached remote villages, ensuring timely treatment for livestock and increasing farmer awareness about modern animal care practices.

With a dedicated team and a vision for a healthier livestock ecosystem, Pasuseva continues to bridge the gap between traditional care and scientific veterinary methods, enhancing both animal health and farmer livelihoods.

Services

Gaushala Loan Yojna

Financial support for farmers to establish and modernize Gaushalas for improved cattle care.

  • Low-interest loans for infrastructure
  • Subsidy on fodder and equipment
  • Regular veterinary support

Bakri Palan Loan Yojna

Empowering individuals with loans and training for profitable goat farming and livelihood.

  • Loan for purchasing goats and sheds
  • Goat healthcare training
  • Market access and support
