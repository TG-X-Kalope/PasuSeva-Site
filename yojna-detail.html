Gaushala Loan Yojna

The Gaushala Loan Yojna is a visionary scheme aimed at supporting rural communities, NGOs, and farmers in building and modernizing Gaushalas (cattle shelters).

The initiative focuses on enhancing animal welfare, creating rural employment, and ensuring sustainable livestock management through structured financial aid and support systems.

Beneficiaries of this scheme can access low-interest loans for infrastructure development, avail subsidies on fodder and cattle-care equipment, and receive support for veterinary services and training programs.

This initiative not only preserves India’s cattle heritage but also uplifts the rural economy by encouraging entrepreneurship around animal husbandry.

Key Benefits

Low-interest loans for Gaushala infrastructure development
Government subsidy on fodder, water tanks, and milking machines
Free/subsidized veterinary services and vaccinations
Inclusion under rural employment and women empowerment schemes

Financial Assistance Overview

1

Loan Amount

Up to ₹25 lakhs available for new Gaushala construction, maintenance, and machinery purchases.

2

Interest Rate & Repayment

Interest rates as low as 4-6% per annum with a flexible repayment period of up to 7 years.

3

Subsidy & Grants

Eligible candidates can receive up to 40% subsidy on project cost through state rural development departments.

4

Eligible Expenses

Loan can cover land development, fencing, cow sheds, milking systems, feed storage, water systems, and solar-powered utilities.

Eligibility & Requirements

Applicants must be Indian citizens aged 21 and above

Individual farmers, NGOs, SHGs, and rural entrepreneurs are eligible

Land ownership or lease document required for Gaushala premises

Basic project report with financial planning and layout plan

Bank account details and Aadhaar-linked KYC
