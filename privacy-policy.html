Privacy Policy

At Pasuseva, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting the data of our users, volunteers, partners, and anyone who interacts with our platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

We collect essential personal details such as name, phone number, address, email ID, and emergency contact during registration or help requests. Additionally, we may collect data related to rescued animals, medical history, and adoption records. This helps us ensure quick response and proper documentation.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Your data enables us to coordinate rescue operations, inform you about service updates, and improve platform functionality. For example, location data helps us dispatch nearby volunteers faster. We also use data analytics to understand service demand and effectiveness in various regions.

3. Volunteer and Partner Data

If you join as a volunteer or partner (NGO, vet, shelter), we may collect ID proof, certifications, and service records. This ensures safety and accountability when interacting with animals and other users. All submitted documents are securely stored and accessible only to authorized team members.

4. Use of Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. Cookies help us remember user preferences, track activity anonymously for analytics, and reduce response time. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function optimally.

5. Third-Party Services

Some services like online payments, maps, and location tracking may be powered by third-party tools. While we choose partners carefully, we encourage users to review their individual privacy policies. Pasuseva does not share unnecessary data unless required for service fulfillment.

6. Data Security Practices

We implement multiple layers of security including encrypted databases, access control, and frequent audits. Only key team members can access sensitive information. We regularly update our systems to prevent data breaches and follow industry-standard protocols for data protection.

7. Children‚Äôs Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 13 years. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If a child‚Äôs information is submitted unknowingly, we urge guardians to contact us for immediate deletion or correction of records.

8. Your Data Rights

You have the right to view, edit, or delete your personal information anytime. If you no longer wish to be part of Pasuseva‚Äôs network, you can request complete data removal by contacting our support team. We comply with Indian IT laws for data privacy and user rights.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be revised occasionally due to legal, technical, or service changes. All users will be notified about major updates via email or on-site announcements. Continued use of our services after updates indicates agreement with the new terms.

10. Contact for Privacy Concerns

If you have any questions or concerns related to this policy or how your data is handled, please reach out at privacy@pasuseva.org. Our support team is available to assist you regarding any data-related queries.
